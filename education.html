EDUCATION
LAND RIGHTS EDUCATION

Above, WOPA education on women land rights


Education is the most effective way to empower women, girls and all other marginalized groups. For any group to realize its potential, they first need to understand their rights and notice their potential.Women need to be educated on their rights to own land; girls need to know about their sexual and reproductive health so as to grow up healthy in the society.Wopa is determined to spread the necessary education to Women, girls and all marginalized groups in the society.



SEXUAL HEALTH EDUCATION

In the photo above, wopa educates girls on their sexual health


We believe that educating girls on their sexual and reproduction health is a key step to abate issues like early pregnancy and spread of sexually transmitted infections among girls and women. We talk to girls on the issue sex- a topic that many parents shy away from. Knowledge is power we believe we are empowering our girls to grow into strong women.


WHICH WAY FOR EDUCATION

We aim to see that all girls and marginalized groups get their formal education.In addition to the formal education,we aim to provide informal education to women, girls and all marginalized groups on topics that are specifically suited for those groups.


OUR PLEA

We plead to the community to help us ensure that every girl and child gets educated to be able to grow int a self-reliant adult. Our girls and children lose their way due to lack of education and necessary knowledge.
