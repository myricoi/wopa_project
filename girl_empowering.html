EMPOWERING GIRLS AND WOMEN
BUILDING AND RAISING TALENT

Above, wopa director with one of the teams in WOPA football tournament for girls


At WOPA, we believe in talents and skills for our girls and women. We therefore aim to nurture and improve talents in our girls so that they can be exploited fully. Ww support activities that spot and help to raise talents. the recent football tournament for girls was just a great success. we also nurture aother talents like singing, dancing and all forms of sporting.



the recent WOPA football tournament for girls (above) was a major success.


The football tournament for girls was supported by different entities and it was an important event for us as it brought together so many groups of girls from different social and economic backgrounds; not just to compete against one another but also to learn from one another, to discover their talents and show their talents.


football team participating at girl's tournament


WOMEN REHABILITATION

As we work so hard to empower our women, we can leave out those that have in one way or the other gone astray. We believe in mistakes and we know women can rise out of fade to become strong pillars in society. Therefore, in our programme, we include women in prison who we want to be rehabilitated then come back to the society as strong vial women.We help in their rehabilitation and follow up on their lives outside bars.


WOPA aids in rehabilitation of female inmates.


In addition to rehabilitation, WOPA also provide support to female inmates such as sanitary towels, toileteries,baby needs for those with babies and also food aid. we also donate television sets to watched by inmates at their free time.


WOPA provide food aid to female inmates.

BASIC SUPPORT FOR GIRLS

We provide donation of sanitary pads and panties to girls from poor backgrounds. We aim to make lives of our girls to be as comfortable as possible so that they can equally compete at school and take on other roles in society without feeling different from the rest.
