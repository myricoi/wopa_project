Above, a walk against Gender Based Violence

THE POSITION OF WOMEN

Above,School girl's choir perform a song about the rights of women.


In the past, women have played as subordinates. The course and direction they have taken in their lives has largely been directed by other people and not themselves. The position that the society has placed women in the past has somewhat disadvantaged them living them with a feeling of always being inferior and vulnerable. This is position is, however, changing gradually as women get empowered. Women are now taking on more roles and surprisingly, they are playing the role very competently. However, as women rise up out of fade, they are met with oppostion from the legal frameworks,economical constraints and tough cultures.
