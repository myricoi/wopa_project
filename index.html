"I do not wish women to have power over men, but over themselves.."
Mary Shelley,

Women Pillar Alliance (WOPA) is an NGO that is actively working towards empowering girls and women in all aspects of life. As the name says, it is an alliance of feminist pillars. A pillar on a building holds the building strong , a pillar in a society keeps the society stable. WOPA is working with women and girls in the society to create pillars that will help girls and women in those societies to live a life of dignity and stability. We are focused on all aspects of a girl/woman’s life ranging from health, social life, economic life and rights. Since the social life of girls and women inevitably involve men, we do not exclude men in helping empower girls and women in the society. We therefore do not overlook issues such as those that arise in marriages and relationships..
